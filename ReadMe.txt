About :
```````

As the commander-in-chief of the Argonia, your task is to protect your space ship from the attack to the cyber-spiders. 
Destroy the enemy fighter spiders (white) using your tank and collect the non-harmful spiders (green & red) in the correct spider buckets for further research on them.

Controls :
``````````

Left/Right Arrow Keys : Movement
Space Bar : Shoot
Left/Right Mouse Button : Rotation of Tank
'r' : Select the red bucket
'g' : Select the green bucket
'b' : select the tank (selected by default)
'p' : pause game
'esc' : quit game

Left Mouse Button : Select cannon/bucket
Right Mouse Button : Rotate cannon

GamePlay :
``````````

Shoot down enemy white spiders to gain 5 points. If a white spider falls on the base of our ship, it destroys that part of the ship and hence our tank cannot cross over that cavity. The buckets (red & green) can move through this destroyed part but will not be seen when crossing the void. 

Collect red and green spiders in the red and green buckets respectively to gain 5 points for each spdier collected.

If you miss collecting a red or green spider, you lose 2 points for each spider lost.

If a spider falls on your tank, you lose the game.

The cannon-balls reflect from the side walls and can be used to kill the white spiders.
